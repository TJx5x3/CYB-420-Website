Islamic Revolutionary Guards Corps

The IRGC operates a sophisticated cyberwarfare division known for its advanced capabilities in cyber espionage, sabotage, and disinformation campaigns. This division has been linked to global operations targeting critical infrastructure, financial institutions, and government agencies. Leveraging a network of state-sponsored hackers, the IRGC seeks to assert Iran's influence and counter perceived adversaries in cyberspace.


Iran's Ministry of Intelligence and Security


Iran's MOIS plays a pivotal role in cyber operations, focusing on intelligence gathering and covert cyber activities. The ministry collaborates closely with other state entities to conduct cyber espionage, targeting dissidents, foreign organizations, and key geopolitical players. MOIS’s efforts are integral to Iran’s strategic objectives, particularly in the domains of surveillance and information warfare.
